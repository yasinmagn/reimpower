About Us

Why We Exist?

With over hundred humanitarian and development Organizations delivering humanitarian and development Programming in Somalia, a need to provide real time consultancy support ranging from quality evidence generation to policy and strategy development is becoming eminent to help institutions deliver their mandate and adapt to prevailing conditions in the context. Reimpower is here in pulling together experts to help fill this priority for our clients to achieve impactful quality and impactful services for Somali people.

Reimpower, established in December 2018, provides a wide range of consulting services in Somalia and Somaliland. Our services include studies and evaluations, monitoring and supervision, third-party monitoring, baseline surveys, impact evaluations, mid-line and end-line project assessments, special studies, research, capacity building, organizational capacity assessments, and translation/proofreading. We specialize in advising stakeholders on designing and implementing evidence-based, contextually appropriate programs for vulnerable populations. Our collaborative, iterative research approach integrates design assessments and desk studies, generating robust evidence syntheses essential for effective, purpose-driven interventions. With a client centric methodology, we deliver high-quality, reliable insights that foster positive change and resilience within communities. Committed to closing the learning loop, we place affected communities at the center of our research and programming efforts. Reimpower is dedicated to empowering local populations and creating sustainable development solutions, making us your trusted partner for meaningful, evidence based programs in Somalia and Somaliland.

We are passionate about leveraging the work of our clients with innovative, technical and experience driven approaches. We are deliberate in our aim to bridge the evidence and knowledge generation gap in the Humanitarian/Development sector with the use of multiple tools and approaches that complement one another.

Vision , Mission and Goals

Vision

A leading social enterprise promoting evidence harvesting, innovation and curated, fit for purpose consultancy services in Somalia and Somaliland

Mission

Create better decision making and impact focused solutions for our clients to design and deliver fit for purpose Interventions that transform societies. We design user centered and management tools including co-creation tools that produce lasting results. This is done through a blended programing that bridge between theory and practice.

Goals

Combing research, evaluations and innovative partnerships with Public and Private entities to inform new economic and social development models that helps move Somali people move out of poverty and inequality.

OUR SERVICES

Third-Party Monitoring

Given the challenging operating environment in Somalia and Somaliland, Reimpower leverages our local presence to provide third-party monitoring services. We collect and share up-to-date program data, enabling our clients to make timely, evidencebased decisions regarding their ongoing initiatives

Environmental and Social Impact Assessments

We identify environmental and social risks, and develop mitigation strategies for development projects across sectors.

Baseline Surveys and Impact Evaluations

We establish robust pre-project baselines through high-quality data collection. This allows us to accurately measure progress and long-term impact of our clients' interventions

Organizational Capacity Assessments

We diagnose skills and system gaps using specialized tools, informing the design of customized capacity building initiatives.

Midline and End-line Evaluations

As programs advance, we conduct thorough midline and end-of-project evaluations to generate evidence on outcomes, capture lessons learned, and inform future programming

Special Studies and Research

Reimpower carries out contextual research and focused assessments to ensure our clients' decisions are grounded in empirical field-level data.

Organizational Capacity and Trainings and Development

We design and deliver customized capacity building initiatives to address the specific needs of our clients' teams and systems.

Translation and Proofreading

In addition to our core technical services, Reimpower provides professional translation and proofreading support across a range of languages.

We have developed a comprehensive study framework and harvested information from various stakeholders and desk study sources to develop a cutting-edge research report about investable business opportunities for youth and women economic empowerment.

Youth Led Mid-Term Evaluations-Work in Progress!
December 2021. Reimpower was commissioned to roll out a youth led evaluation about the Work in Progress! Project and has used talented Somali youth researchers to roll out a youth sensitive evaluation aimed at garnering the project progress through a rigorous and iterative data collection and consultation steps with project stakeholders. The project focuses on creating economic empowerment opportunities for young people in Somalia and does this through a multiple and complementary results (Skills development, entrepreneurship and advocacy and influencing for an enabling employed ecosystem.

July 2021

Report writing Training for Local Implementing Partner in humanitarian nexus.

March 2021

Challenging Harmful Attitudes and Norms for Gender Equality in Somalia. April 2021.
The study focused on assessing the structural and social norms related barriers that impede gender inequality in Somalia.

June 2020

Youth-UP Mid-Term Evaluations, Mercy-Corps, June 2020.
Reimpower had recently carried out a mid-term evaluation about the intermediary impact of youth Shift Project implemented by Mercy Corps to the adolescents and youth in Somalia and Somaliland. This is a multiyear project aimed at creating economic opportunities for youth and women and more specifically, youth from disadvantaged households who lost their income due to harsh climatic conditions and economic inequalities in cities.

June 2020

Evidence Synthesis on Matters Affecting Young People’s Access to financial investments.
Reimpower carried out extensive research on matters affecting young people’s access to financial investments and developing a road map towards increasing young people’s access to financial opportunities including access to loans/investments from financial institutions in Somalia. This research was conducted in June 2020. The research clearly pinpointed key cultural, contextual and financial barriers affecting young people’s availability in the investment chain with more women being at the receiving end than men. Recommendations were also put forward as to how to create sustainable collaboration and synergy with existing financial schemes including small micro-finances run by existing community institutions and banks.

February 2020

Mapping out Youth Policies and women’s empowerment policies and Strategies in Somaliland.
This is a mapping exercises for SOS with the purpose of identifying existing desk strategies and policies affecting young women’s economic empowerment

August 2021

This baseline evaluation was commissioned to inform initial livelihood conditions of target communities for the project.
The assessment provided substantive baseline information by looking at different livelihood indicators including income per day, available livelihood assets, market conditions, community resilience level and potential actors rolling out livelihood interventions in the country.
